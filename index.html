All India Shri Shivaji Memorial Society's Polytechnic

AISSMS Polytechnic, Pune (est. 1994) is a premier, AICTE-approved co-educational institute affiliated with MSBTE, offering top-tier diploma courses in various engineering branches with a focus on practical, industry-oriented technical education in the heart of the city.
